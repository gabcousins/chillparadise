About Us

The Chill Paradise Mallu Discord server is an unique
online community where people with a connection to the Indian
state of Kerala come together. This digital space acts as a
cosy meeting place where individuals share their love for
Kerala's traditions, language, and culture and different
Indian traditions and this is not just a place for keralites,
All of you are welcome in this server regardless of your
nationality or your background. We hope you enjoy the server
and have a great time...
